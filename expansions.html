List of Pokémon Trading Card Game expansions

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.
Jump to navigationJump to search

This is a list of English Pokémon cards from the Pokémon Trading Card Game expansions. For Japanese sets, see the list of Japanese Pokémon Trading Card Game expansions.

Prior to the EX Series, expansions were distributed by Wizards of the Coast. Starting with EX Ruby & Sapphire, expansions were produced by Pokémon USA and distributed by Nintendo of America.[1] In 2006, distribution responsibilities were transferred to Pokémon USA.[2]
